Black Sea Port
Key Ports and Logistics Hubs of the Black Sea Region

Strategic Importance

The Black Sea serves as a major corridor for grain, metal products, and container exports from Ukraine and Europe. The region’s ports are deeply integrated into European transport networks (TEN-T).
